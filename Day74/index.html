The Cricket World Cup is an international tournament held every four years, featuring teams from around the world competing in the One Day International (ODI) format. Established in 1975, it is one of the most prestigious events in cricket. Teams play a series of matches, leading to knockout stages, with the final determining the champion. The tournament has witnessed memorable moments and performances, making it a highlight in the cricketing calendar.
